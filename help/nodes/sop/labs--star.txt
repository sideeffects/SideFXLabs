""" Create a simple star primitive. """

This node creates a simple star primitive.

Replaced by _Labs Simple Shapes SOP_. To be removed in Houdini 20.5.

@parameters
    Orientation:
        Specifies the plane on which the generated primitive lies.
    Radius:
        Controls the radius of the peak and valleys of the star.
    Center:
        Controls the location of the star.
    Rotate:
        Sets the rotation of the generated star.
